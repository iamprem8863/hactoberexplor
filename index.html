Rail Coach Factory

Established in 1986, RCF is a coach manufacturing unit of Indian Railways. It has manufactured more than 30000 passenger coaches of different types including Self Propelled passenger vehicles which constitute over 50% of the total population of coaches on Indian Railways. Its a production unit with 1025 coaches target approx. Per year.

Santa Monica

Santa Monica is known for its beaches, clubs, and restaurants. The first and foremost answer has to be the beaches. ... The Pier is probably the second reason Santa Monica is known. The pier is a lot of fun, it is iconic, and there is lots of things to do on it.
